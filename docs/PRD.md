# WorthIt - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Version**: 1.0.0
**Last Updated**: June 2026
**Status**: MVP Development

---

## Executive Summary

WorthIt is an AI-powered spending decision assistant for Gen Z and young adults that predicts whether a purchase should happen before money is spent.

## Target Market

- College students (18-24)
- Young professionals (22-32)
- First-time earners
- People struggling with impulse spending

## Core Features - MVP

1. Authentication (Google OAuth + Email)
2. Financial profile setup
3. "Should I Buy This?" AI calculator
4. Expense tracking
5. Budget management
6. Dashboard with insights
7. Basic AI chat

## Monetization

- Free: $0 (3 AI decisions/month)
- Student: $4.99/mo (unlimited)
- Premium: $9.99/mo (advanced features)
- Family: $19.99/mo (5 members)

## Success Metrics

- 10K users in month 1
- 50+ NPS score
- 5% conversion to paid
- 70% monthly retention
